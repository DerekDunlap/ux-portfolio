Project Overview

GuardianVets operates as a 24/7 tele-triage and digital front desk for veterinary practices — managing intake, guiding medical triage, supporting after-hours calls, and scheduling appointments with a team of highly trained remote Certified Vet Technicians. They really are the lifeline between clinics and the pet owners who rely on them.

“8 years ago, I adopted dog Patrick, had an issue with him over the weekend. Contacted his vet and got a voicemail that said go to an ER or call on Monday.” -John, CEO & Founder

Industry Research

To understand the landscape, we looked into industry struggles and opportunities. We learned that as veterinary tele-health grows at a projected 16% CAGR, GuardianVets can expand its role as a first touch point for urgent care support, advice and triage. At the same time, clinics are facing staffing shortages, rising call volumes, and overwhelmed front desks—making reliable triage support not just helpful, but essential.

  • Increased pressure on clinics to retain clients
  • Greater reliance on triage services
  • After-hours triage offers a way to prevent unnecessary and expensive emergency vet visits.
